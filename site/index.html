Bachelor of Business Entrepreneurship - BBus (ENTR)

The purpose of this document is to discuss the idea, philosophy, rationale and structure of the course of study - Bachelor of Business (ENTR). An integrated curriculum framework is presented. This framework is informed by and aligned with the SydneyMet’s Strategic intentPlan , Graduate Attributes (GAs) and the AQF level 7 requirements. Appropriate Course Learning Outcomes (CLOs), the duration and volume of learning, and a set of 29 Units of Study included in the Course are also discussed.
